by Small Business Administration (SBA)

SBA loan deadline: March 10, 2025

The SBA has many resources to help small businesses and
              entrepreneurs. This includes financing, training, and advocacy.

by Employment Development Department (EDD)

EDD offers services to support employers impacted by disasters.
              This includes disaster relief payments, extensions for payments,
              and payroll reports.

by California Office of the Small Business Advocate (CalOSBA)

CalOSBA offers resources and support for businesses and workers
              impacted by the wildfires. This includes financial help, recovery
              programs, and workforce support.

by City and County of Los Angeles

LA County businesses and nonprofits can get cash grants from
              $2,000 to $25,000. Submit your application by March 12.

by Franchise Tax Board (FTB)

Businesses impacted by the wildfires may postpone filing and
              paying income taxes. The deadline for them is now October 15,
              2025. Only Los Angeles County was granted postponement. If the
              principal place of business is outside of Los Angeles County, you
              must file and pay by the normal due dates.

by CA Department of Tax and Fee Administration (CDTFA)

Get emergency relief from penalties and interest. You can also get
              copies of tax records and up to a 3- month filing extension on
              your taxes.
